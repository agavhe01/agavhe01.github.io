This dashboard visualizes NYPD crime data for 2024, breaking down arrests by offense class, borough, and perpetrator demographics. Users can explore monthly arrest trends and the prevalence of different offense types to understand crime patterns across New York City.

Technical Overview

  • Data Preparation and Cleaning: Python
  • Dashboard and Visuals: Tableau

Description

This dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of the 2024 New York City Police Department Crime Report. It features key metrics like total arrests (259,067), broken down by offense class (Misdemeanor, Felony, etc.), and allows users to filter data by offense class, borough, perpetrator race, and sex. A timeline chart shows the number of arrests per month, highlighting trends and fluctuations throughout the year, while a list of offense types with their percentages offers insight into the most common crimes. The dashboard aims to provide a clear picture of crime demographics and patterns in NYC for informed decision-making and community awareness.
