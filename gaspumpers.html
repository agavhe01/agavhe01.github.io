Dark Mode

Gas Pumpers

Unity Game where the objective is to deliver as many fuel and snack items to the cars, while avoiding getting hit by traffic and avoiding an enemy stalker.

Billsplit

  • Role: Developer
  • Source: https://github.com/Snw125/CS23-Team10-Midterm
  • Website: https://snowysnw125.itch.io/gaspumpers
You are a rebel gas station attendant who needs to service as many cars as possible before they leave their stations. Bring items from the inventory and drop them off at the cars. Make sure to look both ways before you cross the street! And watch out for your rival gas station attendant

Technical Overview

  • Stack: Unity Game Engine, C#, Blender3D, Itch

Game Functionality

Players must deliver snack and gas items to cars before the respective car's time bar completely depletes, at which point the car leaves and the player gets no point.
